🖥️ Install Beam as a PC App

You can install Beam as a Progressive Web App (PWA) on your PC — it sits in your taskbar like a real app with no browser UI. Works on Windows, Mac, and Linux.

1

Open Beam in Chrome or Edge on your PC.

2

Click the install icon in the address bar (screen with a ➕), or go to Menu → Install Beam.

3

It launches in its own window — pin it to your taskbar for one-click access.

⚡ Self-Host Locally

For the fastest transfers, save this HTML file and serve it on your local network. Both devices must be on the same WiFi.

1

Save this page as beam.html

2

In a terminal, navigate to the folder and run:

python3 -m http.server 8080
3

On your phone, open http://[your-PC-local-IP]:8080/beam.html

💡 Find your local IP with ipconfig (Windows) or ifconfig (Mac/Linux)

🔗 Works Anywhere

WebRTC punches through NAT — so Beam works across different networks too, not just local WiFi.
